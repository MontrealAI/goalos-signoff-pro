# GoalOS Signoff Pro v40 — Autonomous AI Concierge & Route Orchestrator

Open `ask-goalos-live.html` after deployment.

v40 upgrades the site chat from a simple navigator into a local-first autonomous concierge:

- ask a question;
- receive a grounded answer;
- see the recommended page or proof object;
- auto-route with a visible cancel option;
- inspect Signoff, Mission 001, v22–v40 labs, the 48-contract atlas, and RSI/ASI governance;
- optionally connect a secure server-side live AI endpoint later.

Public default: local-first, no uploads, no cookies, no analytics, no wallets, no payments, no external AI calls, no value moved.

Important: because a chat field exists, do not describe v40 as “no text inputs.” The accurate boundary is: local question input, no transmission by default.
