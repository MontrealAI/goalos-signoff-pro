# Commercial Product Specification

## Product

**GoalOS Signoff** is the acceptance layer for AI-assisted work.

It gives clients and builders a shared, auditable record of:

- what was requested;
- what was delivered;
- what evidence supported the delivery;
- what a reviewer recommended;
- what the client accepted or rejected;
- which exact artifact versions were part of the final decision.

## Target customer

Start with customers who already buy or deliver AI-assisted work:

1. AI consultants
2. Small AI agencies
3. Software contractors
4. Research and strategy teams
5. Enterprise AI-pilot owners

## Why this product is commercially useful

Mainstream customers do not initially want staking, wallets, or smart-contract complexity. They want fewer disputes, clearer handoff, and a reliable record of acceptance.

GoalOS Signoff solves that exact problem while preserving a path to later protocol features.

## Jobs to be done

### Client

> I need to know whether the AI-assisted deliverable actually met the brief before I approve it.

### Builder

> I need a clean record that the client accepted the exact version I delivered.

### Reviewer

> I need a structured way to say what passed, what is missing, and what remains uncertain.

## First customer promise

> Know when AI work is actually done.

## Core product loop

1. Client creates a brief.
2. Builder submits work and evidence.
3. GoalOS checks completeness and integrity.
4. Reviewer recommends accept / changes / reject.
5. Client makes the final decision.
6. GoalOS issues a signed Mission Receipt.
7. The receipt can be attached to an invoice, audit file, or project record.

## Differentiation

GoalOS Signoff is not a generic project-management tool. It is designed around acceptance evidence:

- acceptance criteria first;
- evidence-to-criterion mapping;
- explicit AI-use disclosure;
- limitations disclosure;
- human final authority;
- signed receipts;
- protocol-ready hashes.

- unlimited Signoffs

- exportable audit history

### Team

- approval policies

### Independent Review

- human reviewer marketplace later
- priced per project

## Future protocol upgrades

After customer demand is proven:

1. Ethereum anchoring of receipt hashes.
2. AGIALPHA-bonded reviewers.
3. Proof-to-payment escrow.
4. Disputes and challenges.
5. Reputation and credential portability.

Do not add these before the ordinary Signoff workflow is genuinely useful.
