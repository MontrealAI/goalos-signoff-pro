> How to sell the first 10 pilots.

---

## Best outreach target

AI consultants and small agencies with repeat deliverables.

## Opening email

```text
Subject: Cleaner client acceptance for AI deliverables

Hi [Name],

I’m piloting GoalOS Signoff, a simple workflow for defining acceptance criteria, collecting evidence, and issuing a signed receipt when AI-assisted work is accepted.

It is designed for consultants and agencies who want fewer “is this done?” conversations at delivery time.

Would you be open to trying it on one real deliverable this month?
```

## Discovery questions

- [ ] How do clients accept work today?
- [ ] Where do handoffs break down?
- [ ] What evidence do clients ask for?
- [ ] How often do change requests happen?
- [ ] Would receipts help with invoicing or trust?

## Demo flow

1. Show a template.
2. Create a brief.
3. Upload sample evidence.
4. Request changes.
5. Accept final version.
6. Show Mission Receipt.
7. Mention optional verification only after they understand the receipt.
