## FAQ snippet

**Is this blockchain-only?**  
No. Most users start with ordinary signed receipts. Blockchain verification is optional.

**Does GoalOS decide whether work is good?**  
No. GoalOS checks completeness and evidence coverage. Humans accept or reject the work.

**Does this hold money?**  
No. v1.1 does not escrow customer funds.
