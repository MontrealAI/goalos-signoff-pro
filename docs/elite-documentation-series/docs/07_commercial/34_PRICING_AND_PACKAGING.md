## Pricing principle

Charge for workflow value first. Do not force crypto usage into the mainstream plan.

## Pilot pricing

| Plan | Suggested pilot price | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 0 | 3 Signoffs/month, basic receipts |
| Professional | $49/month | Unlimited personal Signoffs, templates, receipts |
| Team | $199–$299/month | Workspace, roles, comments, team templates |
| Independent review | $99–$299/project | Human review add-on |
| Verified receipt add-on | $5–$15/receipt or bundled | IPFS/Sepolia initially, Mainnet later only after approval |


## What to avoid

- Charging in AGIALPHA to mainstream customers on day one
- Escrow fees before custody/legal readiness
- Complex per-seat pricing before product-market evidence
- Unlimited storage without retention limits

## Billing copy

> Start free. Upgrade when Signoff becomes part of your delivery workflow.
