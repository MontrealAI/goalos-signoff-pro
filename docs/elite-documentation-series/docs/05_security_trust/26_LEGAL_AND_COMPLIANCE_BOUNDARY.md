# Legal and Compliance Boundary

**Product:** GoalOS Signoff Pro v1.1  
**Audience:** GoalOS team, pilot customers, operators  
**Owner:** GoalOS product and operations  
**Status:** Ready for private beta  
**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Updated:** 2026-06-26T00:05:06Z

> Clear boundaries so the product is not oversold.

---

## Product boundary

GoalOS Signoff records acceptance decisions. It does not replace contracts, legal advice, regulated audits, accounting opinions, or professional certifications.

## Terms should state

- GoalOS does not independently verify every factual statement
- Users are responsible for their own acceptance decisions
- AI assistant outputs are informational only
- Receipts record process and evidence; they do not guarantee correctness
- Optional blockchain anchoring publishes hashes, not private files
- No escrow or custody in v1.1

## Before adding escrow

- [ ] Payments/custody legal review
- [ ] Dispute terms

- [ ] Sanctions screening approach
- [ ] Tax/accounting treatment
- [ ] User-fund authorization
- [ ] Incident reserve/insurance assessment
