# Risk Register

**Product:** GoalOS Signoff Pro v1.1  
**Audience:** GoalOS team, pilot customers, operators  
**Owner:** GoalOS product and operations  
**Status:** Ready for private beta  
**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Updated:** 2026-06-26T00:05:06Z

> Prioritized risks and mitigations.

---

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Users do not understand why receipts matter | High | Lead with acceptance pain; use examples |
| Workflow feels too heavy | High | Templates, fewer fields, concierge onboarding |
| AI assistant overclaims | High | Conservative copy and human authority line |
| Private evidence exposed | Critical | RLS tests, access reviews, no public IPFS by default |
| Receipts treated as legal certification | Medium | Clear terms and boundary language |
| Blockchain distracts mainstream users | Medium | Make verification optional and advanced |
| Pilot users do not repeat | High | Interview, reposition, improve workflow before scaling |
| Escrow rushed too early | Critical | Separate roadmap gate and legal/security review |
