## Board summary

GoalOS Signoff Pro v1.1 turns AI-assisted deliverables into structured acceptance workflows with signed receipts.

## Why it matters

AI increases output volume but creates trust and acceptance ambiguity. GoalOS can own the acceptance layer.

## Strategic wedge

AI consultants and agencies with recurring client deliverables.

## Differentiation

- Acceptance workflow rather than generic project management
- Evidence-to-criteria mapping
- Signed Mission Receipts
- Optional public verification
- Future AGIALPHA-secured review and settlement

## Pilot target

10 attempted Signoffs, 7 completed, 3 repeat-intent users.

## Investment thesis

If users value receipts and change-request workflow, GoalOS can expand into reviewer networks, verified receipts, bonded review, and proof-to-payment.
