## Pilot invitation

```text
Subject: Invitation to try GoalOS Signoff

Hi [Name],

We are inviting a small group to test GoalOS Signoff, a workflow for accepting AI-assisted deliverables with evidence and signed receipts.

The pilot is lightweight: use it for one real deliverable, tell us what was confusing, and share whether you would use it again.
```

## Change request support macro

```text
A change request means the work is not accepted yet. The reviewer identified specific criteria that need more evidence or revision. Please update the submission and resubmit.
```

## Receipt explanation macro

```text
A Mission Receipt records what was accepted, which evidence was attached, who accepted it, and when. It helps both sides avoid confusion later.
```
