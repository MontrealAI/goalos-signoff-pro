---

## Roadmap principle

Start with useful acceptance workflow. Add trust rails only when users ask for them and the safety gates are satisfied.

## Product ladder

| Stage | Product | Customer promise | Do not launch until |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Signoff Basic | Acceptance workflow and signed receipts | Private beta app passes security and UX tests |
| 2 | Team Signoff | Templates, comments, revisions, organizations | At least 7 pilot completions |
| 3 | Verified Receipts | Optional IPFS/Ethereum anchoring | Receipt verification UX is clear |
| 4 | Bonded Review | Reviewers stake `$AGIALPHA` | Contracts remediated and audited |
| 5 | Proof-to-Payment | Accepted work releases payment | User-fund authorization, custody/legal review, dispute operations |
| 6 | Protocol marketplace | Reusable reputation and settlement rails | Liquidity, governance, and abuse controls proven |
