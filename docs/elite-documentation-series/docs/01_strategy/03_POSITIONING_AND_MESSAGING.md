# Positioning and Messaging

**Product:** GoalOS Signoff Pro v1.1  
**Audience:** GoalOS team, pilot customers, operators  
**Owner:** GoalOS product and operations  
**Status:** Ready for private beta  
**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Updated:** 2026-06-26T00:05:06Z

> How to explain GoalOS Signoff Pro without overcomplicating it.

---

## Primary positioning

**GoalOS Signoff is the acceptance layer for AI work.**

## Do say

- Know when AI work is done
- Turn deliverables into signed Mission Receipts
- Define acceptance criteria before work starts
- Collect evidence before approval
- Keep a record of what was accepted
- Optionally verify important receipts publicly

## Do not lead with

- 48 contracts
- AGIALPHA
- AGI/ASI claims
- proof-settled machine economy
- tokenomics
- Mainnet activation
- escrow

Those belong in advanced documentation, not first-touch customer messaging.

## Homepage headline options

- Know when AI work is actually done.
- Accept AI work with evidence, not guesswork.
- Define done. Check evidence. Sign off with confidence.
- The acceptance workflow for AI-assisted delivery.

## Subheadline

> Create a clear brief, collect the deliverable and evidence, request changes, accept the final version, and issue a signed Mission Receipt.

## Trust line

> No wallet required. No escrow. Optional public verification when you need it.
