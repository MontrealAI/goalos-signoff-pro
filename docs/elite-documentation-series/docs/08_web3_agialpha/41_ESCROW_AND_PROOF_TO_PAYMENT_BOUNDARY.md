> Why settlement is powerful later but unsafe to rush.

---

## Why not escrow now

Escrow introduces:

- Custody-like risk
- Dispute operations
- Legal and compliance complexity
- Smart-contract exploit risk
- Customer support burden
- Accounting and refund workflows

## Future proof-to-payment flow

```text
Brief defined
Reward funded
Builder submits evidence
Reviewer approves
Client accepts or dispute resolves
Payment releases
Receipt issued
```

## Required gates

- [ ] Legal review
- [ ] External smart-contract audit
- [ ] Operational monitoring
- [ ] Emergency pause/recovery
- [ ] Dispute staffing
- [ ] Clear terms
- [ ] Insurance or reserve analysis
- [ ] User-fund authorization

## Interim alternative

Use receipts to support invoices and off-platform payment. This delivers value without custody.
