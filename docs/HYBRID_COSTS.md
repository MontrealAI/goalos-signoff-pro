# Costs for GoalOS Signoff Hybrid

## Core SaaS costs

- hosting
- database
- private storage
- transactional email
- AI-assisted checks, if enabled
- monitoring and backups
- customer support

## Blockchain costs

Anchoring one receipt uses a normal Ethereum transaction. On Sepolia, this uses test ETH. On Mainnet, it uses real ETH. GoalOS can sponsor gas so customers do not need wallets.

## No customer crypto requirement in v1

The customer can pay by ordinary SaaS billing. They do not need AGIALPHA, ETH, MetaMask, or token approvals.

## Future costs

AGIALPHA staking and escrow introduce security, audit, operations, and legal costs and should not be activated until separate readiness gates pass.
