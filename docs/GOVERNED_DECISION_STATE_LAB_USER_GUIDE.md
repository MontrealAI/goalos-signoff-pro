# Governed Decision State Lab — User Guide

The lab shows the central GoalOS product idea: the deliverable is not a report; it is a governed decision state.

Open `governed-decision-state-lab.html`, choose a scenario, and press **Run decision state**. The browser-local demonstration walks through mission contract, claims matrix, contradiction register, verifier report, risk ledger, decision certificate, action graph, Chronicle rule, and capability package.

The page is synthetic and public-safe. It does not ask for files, text, credentials, wallet connection, or personal data.

The most important lesson:

```text
AI output can look persuasive.
GoalOS makes acceptance inspectable.
```
