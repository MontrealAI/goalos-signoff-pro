# Hybrid launch playbook

## Stage 1 — Commercial private beta

Launch Standard Signoff only.

Goal:

```text
10 real Signoffs attempted
7 completed
3 customers say they would use it again
0 lost evidence
0 broken receipts
```

## Stage 2 — Sepolia Verified Receipts

Enable on-chain anchoring on Sepolia for internal and friendly users.

Goal:

```text
10 accepted receipts anchored
10/10 hashes verify
0 relayer errors
0 customers asked to buy crypto
```

## Stage 3 — Paid Verified Receipts

Offer Verified Receipt add-on to selected paid customers. GoalOS sponsors gas.

## Stage 4 — Mainnet anchoring

Only after production review, contract verification, monitoring, key-management policy, and cost controls.

## Stage 5 — AGIALPHA-secured review

Only after independent audit and protocol governance approval.

## Stage 6 — Proof-to-payment

Only after explicit user-fund authorization, escrow security review, customer contracts, accounting, and support operations.
