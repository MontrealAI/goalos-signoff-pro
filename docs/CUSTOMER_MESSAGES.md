# Customer Messages

## Homepage headline

Know when AI work is actually done.

## Subheadline

Define the brief, collect the evidence, obtain human approval, and issue a signed Mission Receipt.

## Short pitch

GoalOS Signoff gives clients and builders a clear record of what was requested, what was delivered, what evidence supported it, and who accepted it.

## For AI consultants

End every client project with a signed acceptance record, not a vague email thread.

## For clients

Approve the exact version of the work only after seeing the evidence mapped to your criteria.

## For reviewers

Give a structured recommendation: accept, request changes, or reject, with clear reasons.

## Trust line

No wallet. No AGIALPHA. No Ethereum transaction. Human final authority.
