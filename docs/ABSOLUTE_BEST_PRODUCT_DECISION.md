# Product decision: hybrid is the best commercial path

The absolute best first product is not pure SaaS and not pure Web3.

- Pure SaaS is easiest to buy, but underuses GoalOS protocol credibility.
- Pure Web3 is aligned with the protocol, but too hard for mainstream customers.
- Hybrid gives mainstream users a normal product and gives serious users verifiable receipts, IPFS portability, and a clean path to AGIALPHA-secured settlement.

## Default mode

No wallet required. No blockchain transaction. Private files stay private. Client controls acceptance.

## Advanced mode

Accepted receipts can be anchored on-chain, exported to IPFS, and later mapped into the 48-contract GoalOS protocol.

## Why this is commercially stronger

It sells an immediate business outcome while preserving the protocol upside.
