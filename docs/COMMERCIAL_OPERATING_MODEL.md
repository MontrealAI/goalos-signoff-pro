# Commercial operating model

## Product promise

Know when AI-assisted work is actually done.

## First market

- AI consultants
- AI agencies
- Research teams
- Software contractors
- Enterprise AI pilot teams

## Customer-visible value

- Cleaner client handoff
- Fewer disputes
- More professional invoices
- Better audit trail
- Clearer acceptance criteria
- Verifiable receipt for exactly what was accepted

## Pricing hypothesis

- Free: 3 Signoffs per month
- Professional: $49/month
- Team: $249/month
- Independent review: $99–$299 per project

## Cost structure

- Hosting
- Database and storage
- Transactional email
- AI checks if enabled later
- Support
- Security monitoring
- Human review operations

No Ethereum gas, AGIALPHA, wallet, or escrow cost exists in Phase 1.

## What to avoid

- Do not market Phase 1 as autonomous proof of factual correctness.
- Do not imply legal finality.
- Do not promise blockchain settlement.
- Do not introduce escrow before security, legal, and operational gates are complete.
