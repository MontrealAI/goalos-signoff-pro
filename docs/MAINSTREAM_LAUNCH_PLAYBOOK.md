# Mainstream Launch Playbook

## Phase 0 — Internal smoke test

Run one full project with your own team:

- Client creates a brief.
- Builder submits a deliverable.
- Reviewer recommends acceptance.
- Client accepts.
- Receipt verifies.

## Phase 1 — Private pilot

Invite 3–5 people who already buy or deliver AI work.

Ask each person to use GoalOS Signoff for one real deliverable.

Do not pitch blockchain. Pitch clarity:

> Before you approve AI work, see what was promised, what was delivered, and what evidence supports it.

## Phase 2 — Paid pilot

Offer the Professional plan manually to the users who completed the pilot.

The goal is not scale. The goal is proof that someone values the workflow enough to pay.

## Phase 3 — Public waitlist

Publish three things:

1. Example Mission Receipt.
2. Customer-friendly demo.
3. A short page explaining “Know when AI work is done.”

## Phase 4 — Protocol integration

Only after the mainstream workflow works:

- optional receipt anchoring;
- reviewer bonding;
- proof-to-payment;
- AGIALPHA utility.
