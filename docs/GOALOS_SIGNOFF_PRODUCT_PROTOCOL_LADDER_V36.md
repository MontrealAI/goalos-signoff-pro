# GoalOS Signoff Product & Protocol Ladder v36

## Product principle

The best first product is not a generic proof generator. It is **GoalOS Signoff**:

> Define what done means, submit AI-assisted work and evidence, obtain human approval, and issue a signed Mission Receipt.

## First customer

AI consultants and small AI agencies delivering research, strategy, automation, or software work to clients.

## Five-screen MVP

1. Create — assignment and acceptance criteria.
2. Submit — deliverable and supporting evidence.
3. Check — completeness, citations, tests, risks, missing evidence.
4. Review — Accepted, Needs Changes, or Rejected.
5. Receipt — brief, evidence, decision, timestamps, limitations.

## Protocol ladder

- Signoff Basic: no wallet, no chain transaction, protocol-compatible receipt.
- Signoff Verified: anchor hashes and issue proof cards/credentials after gating.
- Signoff Secured: reviewer bonds, challenge windows, reputation, slashing-ready accountability.
- Signoff & Settle: payment releases only after accepted proof and dispute rules.

## Boundary

The current public site is a demo and documentation surface. It does not move value, connect wallets, authorize user funds, or claim production settlement.
