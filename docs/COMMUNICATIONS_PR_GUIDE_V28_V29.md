# Communications and PR Guide — v28/v29

## Iconic line

**Blockchain proves the transaction. GoalOS proves the work.**

## Standard

**No Proof. No Trust. No Settlement.**

## User-friendly explanation

Blockchain can show that value moved. It cannot, by itself, prove that the grant was earned, the milestone was completed, the audit issue was fixed, the upgrade was safe, or the AI agent did the work. GoalOS adds the missing proof layer before trust, reputation, governance, or settlement readiness escalates.

## Best public claim

GoalOS Signoff Pro gives blockchain projects a proof-to-acceptance standard: define the mission, collect the evidence, replay the work, validate the claim, preserve human authority, issue the receipt, and only then consider settlement readiness.

## Audience hooks

- DAOs: no grant payout without a proof package.
- Auditors: no remediation closeout without replayable evidence.
- Investors: no serious diligence without a Mission Receipt.
- Enterprises: no partner approval without evidence, risk, and signoff.
- AI agents: no settlement signal without a ProofBundle.

## Boundaries

This is a public-safe demonstration and institutional standard. It does not move value, custody funds, connect wallets, provide legal/financial advice, or guarantee project success.
