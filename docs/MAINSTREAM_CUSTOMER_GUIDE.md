# Mainstream customer guide

## What to say

GoalOS Signoff helps teams accept AI-assisted work with evidence.

## What not to say

Do not lead with AGI, tokenomics, smart contracts, Mainnet, or decentralized settlement.

## Best opening sentence

"Before you approve AI work, GoalOS shows what was promised, what was delivered, what evidence supports it, and what you are accepting."

## First template to sell

AI research and strategy deliverables.

Why: customers already pay for reports and often worry about citations, hallucinations, scope gaps, and ambiguous approval.

## First pilot offer

"We will configure three Signoff templates for your team and run five real project handoffs."

## Success metric

A successful pilot means at least two users say they would use GoalOS again for paid work.
