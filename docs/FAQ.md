# FAQ

## Is the public site a live wallet or payment app?

No. Public demos do not connect wallets, approve tokens, broadcast transactions, take payments, release escrow, or move value.

## Does Signoff Pro provide legal acceptance in every jurisdiction?

No. It demonstrates proof-governed acceptance workflows and signed/replayable receipts. Legal effect depends on context, contracts, and human/legal review.

## Are the labs collecting my data?

No. Public labs are browser-local demonstrations with sample artifacts and no public upload/intake surface.

## Does the repository claim completed AGI or ASI?

No. It describes proof infrastructure for AI work and keeps strong claims bounded by evidence and replay.

## What should reviewers inspect first?

Start with `README.md`, `docs/DEMO_CATALOG.md`, generated manifests in `site/`, and verification scripts in `scripts/`.
