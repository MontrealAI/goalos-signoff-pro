## Chapters

1. **Proof-gated work (v22-v27):** action authority, proof-carrying artifacts, replay, ProofZero planning, mission foundry, and process-resolved evidence.
2. **Blockchain credibility (v28-v30):** blockchain can prove a transaction, but GoalOS proves the work and creates a proof-before-settlement standard.
3. **User-facing proof consoles (v31):** role-based guided console for executives, reviewers, DAO stakeholders, and public reviewers.
4. **Loop → RSI → ASI governance (v32-v35):** deterministic invention operations, dossiers, councils, rollback, and ASI-readiness gates.

## Public-safe boundary

All labs are browser-local demonstrations with no forms, no text inputs, no uploads, no cookies, no analytics, no wallets, no payments, no external AI calls, no personal data, and zero value moved.
