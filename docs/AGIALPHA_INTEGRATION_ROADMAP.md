# AGIALPHA integration roadmap

AGIALPHA should not be required for the mainstream v1 product.

The commercially strongest sequence is:

1. **Signoff Core** — paid SaaS with signed receipts.
2. **Verified Signoff** — chain anchoring sponsored by GoalOS.
3. **Secured Review** — reviewers stake or bond AGIALPHA after contract remediation and audit.
4. **Proof-to-Payment** — escrow and settlement after explicit user-fund authorization.

## Future AGIALPHA utility

- reviewer bonds,
- builder bonds,
- challenge stakes,
- protocol receipt fees,
- credentials,
- reputation incentives,
- settlement security.

## What is not claimed

This package does not claim yield, revenue share, token-price support, guaranteed demand, external audit, production activation, or user-fund authorization.
