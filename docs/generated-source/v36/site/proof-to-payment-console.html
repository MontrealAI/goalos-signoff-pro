Do not force all 48 contracts into every Signoff.

The protocol is modular. A basic Signoff can stay off-chain. Verified Signoff can anchor hashes. Secured Signoff can add bonds and challenges. Signoff & Settle can release payment only after accepted proof and dispute rules.

$AGIALPHA is the security and settlement asset.
