# Pricing and Monetization

This product should make money as conventional software first. Do not require AGIALPHA, ETH, or wallets for mainstream customers.

## Recommended first pricing test

| Plan | Price | Included |
|---|---:|---|
| Free | $0 | 3 Signoffs/month, downloadable receipts |
| Professional | $49/month | Unlimited Signoffs for one consultant or operator |
| Team | $249/month | Shared workspace, reviewer templates, audit history |
| Independent Review | $99–$299/project | Human review as an add-on service |

## Why this is better than forcing tokens first

- Customers understand subscriptions and project-review fees.
- Stable pricing avoids token-price confusion.
- The core workflow can be validated before settlement complexity.
- AGIALPHA can later secure reviewers and disputes rather than act as a login tax.

## Manual billing for the first pilot

This package does not require Stripe on day one. Start with manual invoices or a simple payment link. When demand is proven, add payment automation.

## What customers pay for

- Fewer delivery disputes.
- A cleaner client handoff.
- A signed record of acceptance.
- Evidence mapped to criteria.
- A better review workflow for AI-assisted work.
