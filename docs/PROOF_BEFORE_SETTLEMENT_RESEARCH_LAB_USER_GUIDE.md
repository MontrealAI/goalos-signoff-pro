# Proof Before Settlement Institutional Research Lab v30 — User Guide

## Purpose

v30 makes the research paper usable as a public standard. It is designed for foundations, DAOs, auditors, investors, enterprises, exchanges, partners, and serious blockchain teams.

## What visitors should understand immediately

Blockchain proves the transaction. GoalOS proves the work.

This means the site is not asking users to trust claims. It shows a repeatable path for asking whether work has a mission, evidence, replay, validation, risk boundaries, human authority, and a signed receipt.

## Use the lab for

- DAO grant diligence
- Treasury payout policy
- Audit remediation evidence
- Protocol upgrade review
- AI-agent work settlement readiness
- RWA or oracle-adjacent claim review
- Investor and partner credibility checks
- Corporate procurement signoff

## What it does not do

It does not use wallet integrations, move funds, provide legal or investment advice, certify external facts, or activate production settlement.
