Search control ≠ outcome authority

OMNI can guide exploration allocation, but cannot admit outcomes. Results still pass through mechanical gates before promotion, release, memory propagation, settlement signal, or public claim escalation.

Move-37 handling: surprise is not authority

A breakthrough is not accepted because it is exciting. High novelty increases skepticism. The result must reproduce, survive stress tests, persist against baselines, and be packaged as a dossier for council review.
