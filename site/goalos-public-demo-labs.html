What the demos prove / do not prove

They prove a deterministic, sample-data explanation of proof gates, receipts, manifests, route integrity, replay boundaries, dossiers, council review, and rollback discipline.

They do not prove attained AGI/ASI capability, live RSI operation, live settlement, wallet support, investment merit, external audit completion, legal status, or deployment authority.
