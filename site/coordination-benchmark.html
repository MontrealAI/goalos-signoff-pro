Scoring rule

Useful work must be verified and bounded.

The synthetic benchmark rewards verified work and proof integrity, then subtracts risk, coordination overhead, and cost.

Verified work

How much of the task becomes claim-bound, reviewable, and decision-ready.

Proof integrity

How complete the evidence, replay path, validator report, and claims matrix are.

Risk

Unbounded or unsupported claims increase risk and lower score.

Coordination overhead

More agents are not better if they create waste.

Cost

Cost matters; wins by overspending are not coordination gains.

Boundary

This is a synthetic reproducibility demo, not empirical SOTA.
