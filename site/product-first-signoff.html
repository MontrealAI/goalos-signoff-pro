Launch wedge

AI Research & Strategy Signoff

The first customer is an AI consultant or small AI agency delivering research, strategy, automation, or software work. The buyer does not want a generic docket generator; they want to know whether the work is ready to accept.

Customer promise

Define done. Prove the work. Accept with confidence.

The Proof Pack becomes the export inside every signoff. The product is the acceptance workflow around it: the brief, evidence, checks, reviewer decision, and receipt.

Five-screen product

Create → Submit → Check → Review → Receipt

01

Create · Set the brief

Define the assignment, success criteria, prohibited claims, reviewer authority, and delivery date.

  • Acceptance criteria present
  • Claim boundaries explicit
  • Reviewer authority defined
MISSION_DEFINED
02

Submit · Submit the work

Attach the report, files, links, screenshots, tests, citations, traces, and supporting evidence.

  • Deliverables enumerated
  • Evidence packet present
  • Source list attached
EVIDENCE_SUBMITTED
03

Check · Check the evidence

Surface missing artifacts, weak citations, freshness issues, uncertainty, risk disclosures, and claim-to-evidence gaps.

  • Claim-source matrix
  • Missing-source warnings
  • Risk and uncertainty notes
CHECKS_READY
04

Review · Approve or request changes

Give the client one simple review link and three possible outcomes: accepted, needs changes, or rejected.

  • Reviewer checklist complete
  • Limitations visible
  • Human decision captured
HUMAN_AUTHORITY_RECORDED
05

Receipt · Issue Mission Receipt

Generate a tamper-evident record of the brief, deliverables, evidence, checks, reviewer decision, timestamps, and unresolved limitations.

  • Receipt hash
  • Replay path
  • Public-safe export

Product ladder

Start simple. Add verified rails only when useful.

Tier 1

Signoff Basic

User sees: No wallet SaaS signoff. Create brief, collect evidence, review, receipt.

Protocol underneath: Protocol-compatible docket and receipt hashes; no public chain action.

$AGIALPHA: None required.

Open tier
Tier 2

Signoff Verified

User sees: Verified receipt with anchored hashes and public verification page.

Protocol underneath: Brief/evidence/receipt commitments anchored after customer authorization.

$AGIALPHA: Sponsored protocol fee only if enabled.

Open tier
Tier 3

Signoff Secured

User sees: Independent reviewer, challenge window, reputation and credential history.

Protocol underneath: Reviewer/evaluator bonds, attestations, challenges, and slashing readiness after audit.

$AGIALPHA: Reviewer/evaluator stake after activation.

Open tier
Tier 4

Signoff & Settle

User sees: Bounded proof-to-payment: accepted work releases reward; disputes pause settlement.

Protocol underneath: Job escrow, reward vault, dispute/appeal rails, treasury routing after user-fund authorization.

$AGIALPHA: Settlement, rewards, challenge stake, and protocol revenue when authorized.
