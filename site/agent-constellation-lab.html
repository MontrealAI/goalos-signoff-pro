ROLE CONTRACT PRINCIPLE

A large agent population becomes an institution when each role carries proof obligations.

The constellation lab is deliberately no-input and public-safe. It teaches the user how planners, builders, validators, risk sentinels, Chronicle memory, and human authority cooperate without asking the visitor to submit data. No forms, No input, No upload, No wallet, No cookies, No analytics, No payments, and no value moved.

WHY THIS MATTERS

The institution is the coordination technology.

A large agent population without proof can create noise, conflict, duplicated work, hidden assumptions, and false confidence. The GoalOS pattern gives every role an evidential purpose: planner commits, builder produces, validator checks, sentinel constrains, Chronicle remembers, and human authority decides.

Coordinator selects roles by evidence state, cost, risk, validator availability, and stopping rule.
