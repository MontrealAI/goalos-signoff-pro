What can I actually do with GoalOS?

Start with a real outcome. GoalOS compiles it into mission, evidence, review, receipt, and route.

Use case 1

AI research report signoff

Turn a report into an acceptance-ready Mission Receipt.

What to type: I delivered an AI research report to a client and need proof it is ready to accept.

GoalOS produces

  • brief and success criteria
  • claim-to-source matrix
  • freshness and missing-source checks
  • reviewer decision path
  • signed Mission Receipt

End-to-end path

  1. Paste the outcome you need accepted.
  2. GoalOS defines done and lists the evidence required.
  3. Reviewer accepts, requests changes, or rejects.
  4. Receipt preserves what was delivered and what remains limited.
