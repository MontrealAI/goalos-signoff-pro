The coordination law

Maximum coordination is not maximum autonomy.

Maximum coordination = maximum verified useful work per unit of risk, cost, latency, and proof debt.

Not more agents

More agents without proof can increase contradiction, duplication, and waste.

Better proof loops

Proof gates decide what may be accepted, remembered, reused, or made economically consequential.

Institutional memory

Only accepted evidence becomes Chronicle-ready memory.
